The Best Dog Grooming Service in Mesa

We are an independently owned and operated business. Located in Mesa, AZ, we provide dog and cat grooming services. Since 1989, we've offered your pet a personal, gentle touch with grooming done by qualified experienced groomers.

When you bring your dog to be groomed, our professional groomers will personally meet with you to discuss your pet's specific needs and recommend just the right grooming style.

Please let our groomers know about any special needs. We offer many specialty shampoo treatments to help your pet's skin and coat remain healthy.

Due to different breeds, condition of coat and time needed to complete services, grooming package prices are determined after your dog is assessed at the pet salon.

Our groomers truly love dogs and really enjoy what they do! Whether your dog needs maintenance cleaning & trimming, seasonal care, or a complete makeover, our experienced groomers are here to help.
