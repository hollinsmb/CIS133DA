How to pick the perfect pet

Picking the right dog breed for you should not be taken lightly. The decision should take many factors into account. When it comes to choosing a pup, it is always crucial to research the dog you are seeking. There are several things to consider.

How To Pick The Right Breed

  • Cost and long-term expenses
  • Energy level
  • Size
  • Working vs. Nonworking breeds
  • Special needs

Dog Breed Selection Checklist

  • Age
  • Coat length
  • Shedding
  • Activity level
  • Trainablity
